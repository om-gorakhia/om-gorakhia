## 💼 What I Do

I sit at the intersection of quantitative finance, machine learning, and business strategy. On any given week I might be engineering a multi-strategy trading system, orchestrating AI agents for healthcare workflows, or running econometric models to size a market opportunity. My work tends to start with a hard business question and end with something that runs in production, not just a notebook.

I work across three core areas. The first is building intelligent systems — agentic AI pipelines, LLM-powered tools, and multi-agent orchestration that automate complex decisions rather than just surface information. The second is quantitative modeling — from financial portfolio optimization using Kelly Criterion and mean-variance frameworks, to econometric demand analysis and ML-based risk scoring. The third is turning all of that into something people can actually use, through Streamlit dashboards, interactive BI reports, and clean APIs that non-technical stakeholders can run with.

I've applied these skills across fintech, healthcare, e-commerce, and supply chain — previously contributing to data intelligence work for companies like Amazon, Walmart, and PayPal, and now pushing further into agentic AI and algorithmic systems through my graduate research at NUS.

## 🛠️ Some Projects I'm Proud Of

**[NSE Algorithmic Trading System](https://github.com/om-gorakhia/nse-algo-trading-system)** — Screens over 2,400 stocks on India's NSE exchange every single day across 10 distinct trading strategies. Each trade goes through multiple validation checkpoints before execution, and the system handles end-to-end risk management autonomously. Built entirely in Python with live market data integration.

**[Synpulse — Agentic AI for Chronic Disease Management](https://github.com/om-gorakhia/synpulse)** — Built for the NUS-Synapxe-IMDA AI Innovation Challenge 2026. This system uses multi-agent AI to help patients manage chronic conditions, combining clinical context awareness with personalized health guidance. A real-world demonstration of agentic AI going beyond chatbots into decision-support.

**[IPL Betting Hedge Calculator](https://github.com/om-gorakhia/sports-bet-portfolio)** — A quantitative portfolio management tool for in-play cricket betting. Uses mean-variance optimization, the Kelly Criterion, and linear programming to calculate real-time hedge positions, with Gemini AI providing live match context. Shows how portfolio theory translates directly to fast-moving probabilistic environments.

**[Loan Default Prediction Engine](https://github.com/om-gorakhia/loan-default-prediction)** — Machine learning model that predicts loan defaults with contextual loss evaluation baked in, meaning the model is optimized for business cost, not just raw accuracy. Ships with a full Streamlit dashboard so credit analysts can explore scenarios without touching any code.

**[E-commerce Warehouse Optimization](https://github.com/om-gorakhia/Ecommerce_Warehouse_Optimization)** — Applies linear programming to the messy reality of multi-warehouse distribution. Minimizes fulfilment costs while satisfying demand constraints across locations. The kind of operations research that sounds academic until you see the cost savings on a real supply chain.
